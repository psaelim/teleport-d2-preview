Reading the trade: Phosphor is rounder and friendlier, metrics are identical across weights, and the React package makes weight a prop. Remix is tighter and more clinical, its Health & Medical set nails our subjects (a true cross, home-heart, heart-pulse), and every glyph is a guaranteed -line/-fill pair. Either one replaces BOTH the Lucide chrome plan and the custom-SVG redraw work with a single dependency; the custom glyphs would retire.
